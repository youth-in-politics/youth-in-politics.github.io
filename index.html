Youth in Politics

Youth in Politics is a nationwide organization dedicated to helping youth explore the current political climate in a constructive and impartial manner.
Members of YiP are introduced to ongoing political issues through healthy debate and discussion.

Visit our agenda.

Welcome to the National Youth in Politics Virtual Conference!

The Annual National Youth in Politics Conference brings together politically active youth from around the country for two days of virtual debates, seminars, and workshops. On April 23rd-24th, join more than 500 participants virtually for two days of debates, presentations, and workshops. You'll have the chance to hone your political prowess, and meet people with a wide range of views on several relevant issues.
