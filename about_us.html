About Us

Learn more about our organization and our virtual conferences!

Youth in Politics has been helping High School students constructively explore politics for the last 23 years. Our typically in person conventions allow for youth from all backgrounds to meet peers with a variety of interests and beliefs. We are a non-profit organization, and look forward to continuing to serve the community in the coming years!

What Our Students Say

The Youth In Politics annual conference is run by veterans in the political field hailing from both sides of the aisle. Our leadership team has become increasingly filled with alumni of the program who maintain the constructive spirit it was first founded with.
